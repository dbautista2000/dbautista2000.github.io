About

The Everything Reviews is a website where you can view my personal reviews on any possible thing that exists, like beverages, food, movies or shows, restaurants, or video games.

Why care about my review?

I don't want anyone to care about my review, as they are opinions and should not be taken as fact. However, there's a benefit reading from Everything Reviews since it provides more personal reviews. This contrasts with reading countless threads of reviews where groupthink or bandwagoning takes place, skewing the fairness and honesty of the reviews. Finally, since these are my personal reviews, they are bot-free reviews!
