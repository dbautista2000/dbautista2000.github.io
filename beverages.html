Beverages Reviews

There isn't much to a beverage besides going into the details of its taste.

Coca-Cola and its Diet Counterparts

Coca-Cola is universal classic. Everywhere you go, there will be Coke. It's best served cold with ice cubes in the cup. This is the best soda I've tasted, meaning Pepsi Cola is certainly not my favorite one out of the two. Coke just has more taste than Pepsi, which tastes more watered down even though it has more sugar (by 2 grams). Coke also variants like Diet Coke and Coke Zero, which are surprisingly different. Each have zero calories and sugar, but Coke Zero tries to keep the taste of the original Coke. I give this soda 5 stars out of 5.

Horchata

Traditional Mexican drink made with cinammon, rice, and milk, mostly served with ice. It is hands down one of the best refreshers to have at whatever time, even on a hot day. I would always get an horchata instead of soda whenever I'm eating at a Mexican restaurant. It's a very versatile drink: you can drink it with different kind of food, like pastries, tacos etc. I give this beverage 5 stars out of 5.
